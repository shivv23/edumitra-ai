# Demo Video Script — EduMitra AI

**Duration:** 3 minutes  
**URL:** https://edumitraai.vercel.app  
**Audio:** Screen recording with voiceover

---

## Scene 1: Landing + Login (0:00 – 0:30)

**Screen:** Open browser → go to edumitraai.vercel.app → shows login page

**Voiceover:**
"250 million Indian students study across different boards and curricula. Every student learns differently, but most platforms treat them all the same. And 53% of students report anxiety — with no connection between how they're feeling and what they're learning.

EduMitra connects both. Let me show you how."

**Action:** Type email and password → click Sign In → lands on dashboard

---

## Scene 2: Dashboard (0:30 – 0:50)

**Screen:** Dashboard visible with mastery stats, streak, quick actions

**Voiceover:**
"This is the dashboard. It shows overall mastery, topics covered, quizzes taken, and streak. On the right is a quick study plan and a wellness card — both in one view.

From here you can start studying, check in on your wellness, or view your progress."

**Action:** Hover cursor over stat cards → click "Start Studying"

---

## Scene 3: Study Room — Chat + Quiz (0:50 – 1:30)

**Screen:** Study page loads, chat interface visible

**Voiceover:**
"The study room is where you talk to the AI tutor. It pulls from NCERT curriculum through a RAG pipeline — so answers are syllabus-aligned, not generic web results."

**Action:** Type "What is quadratic equation" in chat → press Enter → response appears

**Voiceover:**
"You can also generate quizzes on any topic."

**Action:** Click "Quiz" button → quiz loads → scroll through questions

**Voiceover:**
"Questions adapt based on your performance. Weak areas get more practice."

---

## Scene 4: Wellness Check-in (1:30 – 2:00)

**Screen:** Navigate to wellness page

**Action:** Click "Wellness" in navbar

**Voiceover:**
"The wellness corner is where students can check in on how they're feeling. It's private, encrypted, and never shares individual data with teachers or parents."

**Action:** Click a mood rating (e.g., 4/5) → response shown

**Voiceover:**
"If someone is in distress, the system flags it and shows helpline numbers — but it never uses AI to diagnose or prescribe. That's intentional."

**Action:** Scroll down to show helpline numbers, breathing exercise

---

## Scene 5: Progress + Teacher/Parent Views (2:00 – 2:40)

**Screen:** Navigate to progress page

**Voiceover:**
"Students can track their mastery across subjects and see recent activity."

**Action:** Show subject breakdown bars, scroll through activity list

**Screen:** Quick switch to teacher view (log in as teacher)

**Voiceover:**
"Teachers see their entire class — who's on track, who needs support, and aggregate wellness trends without exposing individual data."

**Screen:** Quick switch to parent view

**Voiceover:**
"Parents get a summary of their child's progress and alerts — academic and wellness — without breaching privacy."

---

## Scene 6: Closing (2:40 – 3:00)

**Screen:** Back to dashboard or landing page

**Voiceover:**
"EduMitra brings learning and wellness into one platform. It's NCERT-aligned, works in 11 Indian languages, and is DPDP Act compliant from day one.

We're team shivamkumar0423. Thanks for watching."

**Action:** Fade out
